About Us

The fan base came to life among four friends who met when they moved to Jönköping and attended a hockey game together. Inspired by their shared love for the sport, they envisioned creating a community for locals and newcomers alike to bond over their passion for hockey. Aiming to provide a welcoming space for everyone to connect and enjoy quality time with like-minded individuals, they set out to establish a vibrant fan group in the city.

Our emblem, the bull, symbolizes the fighting spirit of HV71. We are a team that never surrenders, always striving to conquer, and embrace the electrifying energy of the exhilarating game nights.
